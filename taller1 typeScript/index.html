Descripcion

Fantasm, also known as Kenzo Meservey, is a techno DJ and producer originally from Los Angeles, United States, and now based in Lyon, France. Renowned for his industrial techno sound, characterised by powerful kicks and dark atmospheres, Fantasm has garnered a devoted following within the techno community.
